### <a name="34-metadata-caching"></a><span data-ttu-id="7aff8-475">3.4 almacenamiento en caché de metadatos</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7aff8-475">3.4 Metadata caching</span></span>

function AmountConsumed(entities Collection([CRONUS_International_Ltd__Zone])) as
